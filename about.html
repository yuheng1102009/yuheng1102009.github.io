Who I Am

I am someone who learns through exploration, adapts through experience, and grows through challenges. My journey is shaped not only by academics, but also by the way I think, reflect, and respond to the world around me.

Thinking Style

I naturally question how and why things work. Instead of accepting information directly, I tend to break ideas down, analyze patterns, and rebuild understanding from first principles. This approach helps me adapt quickly to unfamiliar problems.

Personal Growth

Growth for me has often come from discomfort — moments where I had to adapt, adjust, or rethink my approach. Over time, I learned that improvement is not linear, but built through consistent reflection and effort.
